Questions

Frequently asked

Why is the setup cost so low?

We're investing in this build because this platform forms the foundation of our community product. 22qMinded is our founding partner and helps us refine the product. In return, you get heavily discounted setup pricing that no other client will receive.

When will it be live?

6 weeks after approval. After just 2 weeks, you'll already have a working MVP to test with real content and real payments.

Can I switch to another platform later?

Yes. All your data — members, videos, content, discussions — belongs to you. We'll assist with export if you ever want to move. No lock-in.

What about my existing webinar buyers?

We'll create a migration flow in Phase 4. Options include credit toward membership, complimentary access for a period, or keeping separate access. We'll design the best approach together.

What counts as a "bug" vs. a "feature request"?

A bug is when something doesn't work as described in this proposal. A feature request is anything new that wasn't in the original scope. When in doubt, we'll discuss it openly.

How does the WordPress integration work?

Your WooCommerce shop handles the sale. When a customer completes a Stripe payment, a webhook automatically creates their account on community.22qminded.com. No manual work — fully automated.

What happens after the 12-month minimum?

The subscription continues month-to-month at the same €149/month rate (locked for life as a founding member). You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
